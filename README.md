Epic Knights of the Seven Kingdoms is an addon for Epic Knights - Armors Shields and Weapons which adds various armors, sigils, weapons from ASOIAF Universe by George R. R. Martin, including content from Game of Thrones, House of the Dragon and Knight of the Seven Kingdoms.
